About Me

A coding student from South Africa

Learning to code has been an incredibly empowering experience for me as a woman. Not only has it opened doors to new career opportunities and financial stability, but it's also given me a sense of confidence and creativity. Coding allows me to bring my ideas to life and solve real-world problems, which is an amazing feeling. Plus, being part of a growing community of women in tech has connected me with inspiring mentors and role models who support and motivate me every step of the way. Overall, coding has been a game-changer for me, and I encourage all women to give it a try! In South Africa, a growing number of women are breaking down barriers and stereotypes in the tech industry by learning to code and pursuing careers in software development. Organizations are providing training, mentorship, and support to help women develop their coding skills and build confidence in their abilities. As a result, more women are taking on leadership roles in tech companies, starting their own businesses, and creating innovative solutions to address social and economic challenges in their communities. By increasing diversity in the tech sector, these women are not only empowering themselves but also contributing to a more inclusive and vibrant digital economy in South Africa.
